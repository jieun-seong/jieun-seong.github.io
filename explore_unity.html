Let's learn and build a world!

The Starting Point

Using the free assets pack -- the starter assets and the Synty Studio Polygon Assets -- I made a simple demo where a character interacts with the objects in the map. I separately added sounds effects and particle effects. I got Polygonal Stuff's Low -Poly Particle Pack and the sound clips from Zapsplat and Nox_Sound's Nature - Essentials. 

The Portal and the Skyboxes

I want the cool door from Howl's Moving Castle, so I made a cube that acts as a trigger to load a different scene, using SceneManager LoadScene. For now, it just opens up the other world; our character is left in the original world. I need figure a way out. 

I also added skyboxes from BOXOPHOBIC's Skybox Extended Shader to give more of the fantasy feel. 

There were subtle changes from the previous video: like the sound effects and the colliders. 

What next?

I need to find a way to teleport the character. 
Add some effects for the portal. 
Make an inventory system. 

Eventually I want to build an open world game like Black Desert but with a cozier and more casual feeling or like Zelda BOTW with more diversity in crafts and stories.
